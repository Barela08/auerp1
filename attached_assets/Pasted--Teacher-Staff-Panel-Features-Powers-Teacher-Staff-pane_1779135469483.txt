# Teacher/Staff Panel Features & Powers

Teacher/Staff panel bhi fully professional aur fully working hona chahiye. Teacher sirf apne assigned students, subjects aur classes manage kar sake.

---

# Teacher Dashboard

Teacher dashboard mein ye sab visible hona chahiye:

* Total Students
* Assigned Subjects
* Attendance Overview
* Pending Exam Forms
* Pending Hall Ticket Approvals
* Notifications
* Recent Activities
* Uploaded Results
* Calendar Updates

Dashboard responsive aur same-to-same professional design mein hona chahiye.

---

# Student Management

Teacher:

* Assigned students ki profiles dekh sake
* Student details verify kar sake
* Student documents view kar sake
* Student signatures view kar sake
* Student status check kar sake

Teacher student ko:

* Edit
* Verify
* Update

kar sake agar permission ho.

---

# Attendance Management

Teacher:

* Subject-wise attendance mark kare
* Date-wise attendance manage kare
* Bulk attendance update kare
* Attendance edit/update kare

Student:

* Subject click karke full attendance history dekh sake

Teacher dashboard mein:

* Low attendance students list
* Attendance percentage tracking

visible hona chahiye.

---

# Exam Form Verification

Student exam form submit karega.

Teacher:

* Full student details check karega
* Fees status verify karega
* Attendance verify karega
* Documents verify karega

Uske baad:

* Approve
* Reject

kar sakega.

## Reject Option

Agar reject kare:

* Reject reason mandatory hona chahiye
* Student ko rejection reason visible hona chahiye
